Our Story

Founded in 1999, Kamalyan Consulting began with a simple mission: to help organizations navigate the complex world of environmental regulations while promoting sustainable practices that benefit both business and nature.

What started as a small consultancy with just three environmental scientists has grown into one of the region's leading environmental consulting firms, with a team of over 40 experts serving clients across Armenia and beyond.

Over the past 25 years, we've completed more than 150 projects, helped restore critical ecosystems, and guided countless organizations toward environmental compliance and sustainability.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, science-based environmental solutions that help our clients achieve their goals while protecting and restoring the natural environment for future generations.

Our Vision

A world where economic development and environmental stewardship go hand in hand, where businesses thrive while ecosystems flourish, and where sustainability is the standard, not the exception.

Our Values

Integrity, scientific excellence, collaboration, and a deep respect for nature guide everything we do. We believe in transparent communication and lasting partnerships with our clients.
