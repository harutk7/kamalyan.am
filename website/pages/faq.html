General Questions

We offer comprehensive environmental consulting services including Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA), water resource management, air quality monitoring, waste management solutions, forest conservation planning, regulatory compliance consulting, and sustainability planning. Our team of experts can guide you through every stage of your environmental project.

Kamalyan Consulting was founded in 1999, giving us over 25 years of experience in environmental consulting. Since then, we've completed more than 150 projects and built a reputation for scientific excellence and reliable service across Armenia and the region.

We work with a diverse range of clients including government agencies, municipalities, private corporations, non-profit organizations, international development agencies, and individual landowners. Our expertise spans across construction, manufacturing, energy, agriculture, and conservation sectors.

Yes, all our senior consultants hold advanced degrees in their respective fields and maintain professional certifications. Our company is ISO 14001 and ISO 9001 certified, and we are proud members of the International Association for Impact Assessment (IAIA).

Services & Process

The duration of an EIA depends on the project's complexity and scope. A simple assessment might take 2-3 months, while more complex projects can take 6-12 months. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific project requirements.

Our typical process includes: 1) Initial consultation to understand your needs, 2) Site assessment and data collection, 3) Analysis and report preparation, 4) Review meetings and revisions, 5) Final documentation delivery, and 6) Ongoing support as needed. We maintain clear communication throughout each phase.

Yes, we offer comprehensive monitoring services including water quality testing, air quality monitoring, soil analysis, and ecological surveys. We can establish long-term monitoring programs tailored to your project's specific requirements and regulatory obligations.

Absolutely. Regulatory compliance is one of our core services. We help clients understand and meet all relevant environmental regulations, obtain necessary permits, prepare compliance reports, and navigate inspections. Our team stays current with all changes in environmental legislation.

Pricing & Contact

Our pricing depends on the scope and complexity of the project. We offer both fixed-price contracts for well-defined projects and time-and-materials billing for ongoing consulting services. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific needs during an initial consultation.

You can request a quote by filling out the contact form on our website, calling us at +374 12 345 678, or sending an email to info@kamalyan.am. Please provide as much detail as possible about your project so we can give you an accurate estimate.
